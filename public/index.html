About Julia

I sell the invisible.

My past years working for IT Software-as-a-Service companies have taught me design is more than just aesthetics. A brand is more than the latest campaign or trendy effect, it is how your customers feel — before and after the sale. I focus on producing assets that appeal on an emotional level, regardless of the medium. We are all human, after all.

I'm a team player.

Throughout my career, I've worked in small agencies, as part of in-house creative teams, and as a solo freelancer. I have led and executed design projects for multi-million-dollar product launches, webinars, international conferences, website launches, and more. I have collaborated with cross-functional teams of copywriters, developers, marketers, and subject matter experts to deliver engaging solutions. Teamwork makes the dream work.

I'm accomplished.

I've created work for B2B and B2C audiences, both within the United States and internationally. My work has helped teams achieve KPIs and drive attendance.

Designs generating 2M+ in sales
Webinars attracting 2K+ participants
eBook achieving 1K+ monthly downloads

But more about me.

While I am committed to quality and attention to detail, I don't like to take myself too seriously. In my spare time I love being taken for walks by my dog, exploring new cities, and keeping up with web design trends and technologies. I'm currently looking for companies that believe in remote work and the pursuit of work-life balance. I am always looking to expand my horizons by taking on new projects, learning new toolsets, and meeting new people. So reach out and drop me a line.
