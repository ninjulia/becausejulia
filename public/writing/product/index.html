Shentel Maestro Whole Home DVR Media Center & More!

Maestro is a whole home gateway which provides DVR and search features for your TV, internet connectivity for your house, plus advanced phone features. It consists of one master box to be placed near your internet connection, and up to five players to be placed near your TVs.

Whole Home DVR

  • Record or view up to 5 shows at once.
  • 500 GB storage for recorded shows.
  • Pause recording in one room, resume in another.
  • Watch any recorded show on any TV connected to a player.
  • Pause and rewind live TV.
Record about 300 Hours of SD shows, or 75 hours of HD shows!

Interactive Guide

  • Amazing and easy to use program guide.
  • Search all content (live and recorded) at once.
  • Filter shows by category or create custom lists.
  • Add a custom scrolling ticker to keep up with sports scores, news, weather and other information, no matter what channel you’re watching.

Internet

  • Connect to the net wirelessly from anywhere in your home.
  • Share content from your laptop onto your TV.
  • Supports 802.11n WiFi.

Telephone

  • See Caller ID information on any connected TV screen.
  • Access your Caller ID history log from any connected TV.
  • Use your existing phone wiring - no need to move your phone jacks.
  • 8 hour battery backup for phone service.

Additional Features

  • Play built-in games on-screen with your remote.
  • Ability to add external hard drive for even more storage.

Quick Facts

  • Supports 5 PlayersMaster box is capable of managing programming for up to 5 TVs.
  • Uses existing wiringThe system can be installed using the coax connections already installed near each TV. It also utilizes your phone wiring. In most cases, no new wiring is necessary.
  • Simple connectionsComputers, laptops, tablets and other Wi-Fi enabled devices can be wirelessly connected to the internet with no cables necessary. If a wired connection is preferred, there are Ethernet ports on the master box.
