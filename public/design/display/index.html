Concept

This digital marketing set consists of six banners for use with Google display ads and Facebook social media marketing. Developed for the fictional band, VHS Actually, to promote their Press Any Key to Continue Summer Tour 2021 based on their latest album of the same name. Artwork from the album was leveraged to further reinforce the connection between the album and the tour.

Facebook advertising.
Standing out in the news feed.

Copy and design work together to target existing fans of the band's page and stand out in news feeds when shared.

Display advertising.
Five sizes for most uses.

Banners were created in the most common (and cost-effective) display ad sizes intended for use with Google Ads. Design elements were resized and in some cases omitted to promote readability but maintain campaign design integrity.
